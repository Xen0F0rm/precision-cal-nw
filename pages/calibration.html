Calibration

Precision Cal Northwest provides calibration services for a wide range of mechanical, temperature, pressure, and electrical devices. We strive to provide a balance between quick turnaround time and exceptional quality requirements. All calibrations include a high-quality certification, traceable to NIST Standards, each at no additional cost. Our technicians are trained, qualified, and driven to provide the highest quality work that can be sent back to the customer with pride. Services performed by Precision Cal Northwest include but are not limited to:  Calipers – Digital, Dial, Vernier, Solar  Indicators – Digital, Dial, Test, Plunger, Lever  Micrometers – Digital, Conventional, Outside, Inside, Depth, Optical  Plug, Wire, Ball, & Pin Gauge Inspection  Height & Thickness Gauges – Digital, Dial  Torque Wrenches & Drivers – in-oz, in-lb, ft-lb  Gauges – Pressure, Vacuum, Conventional, Digital  Multimeters – Digital, Analog, Clamp-on  Thermometers – Thermocouples, RTD Probes, Bi-metal, Infrared  Levels/Protractors – Machinists, Spirit, Digital  Bore Gauge Assemblies  Backflow Test Kits
