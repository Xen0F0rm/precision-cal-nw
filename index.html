What We Do

Precision Cal Northwest is a full-service metrology lab providing instrument testing, calibration, repair, and certification. Electronic, mechanical and dimensional instruments are tested to ensure they perform reliably within manufacturer specifications.

Why We Do It

Calibration is a quality assurance measure. It ensures that the instruments and tools used to execute critical tasks are precise and accurate. Calibration provides confidence in the tools and equipment and ensures that the job is done right the first time.

NIST Traceability

Precision Cal Northwest certifies that all serviced instruments will be inspected, calibrated, or compared with master reference standards and meet or exceed all published specifications using standards whose accuracies are traceable to the U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), or other Standard Organizations.
